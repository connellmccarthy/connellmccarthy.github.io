Environmental Impact Promise

Much of my photography is based on my love of nature and the great outdoors. As such, it's a mission of mine to do what I can to protect our beautiful wilderness.

In a small effort to help support the environment, I've partnered with One Tree Planted to assist in global reforestation projects. $10 from every print sold will go towards planting trees across the globe. According to One Tree Planted, each tree is $1, so for every print that you purchase 10 trees are planted.

Additionally, if you make your purchase using Shop Pay, your order will be carbon offset.

Packaging and Shipping

Keeping the integrity of your print safe during transport is something I don't take lightly. My prints are shipped in a range of packaging types depending on the size/variant of the item.

Poster tube

Rigid spiral-wound cardboard tubes in order to keep your photo print from being warped or torn during transport.

Envelope

Highly opaque, rigid postal envelopes for the smaller photo prints that don't take up a lot of space.

Double-walled carboard box

Rigid, double-wall corrugated cardboard boxes ensuring maximum protection when shipping photo prints that include a frame.
